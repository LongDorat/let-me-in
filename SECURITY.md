# Security Policy

## Reporting A Vulnerability

Please do not open public issues for security vulnerabilities.

Report vulnerabilities privately by contacting the maintainers through your preferred private channel and include:

- A description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Suggested remediation (if available)

## Response Expectations

Maintainers aim to acknowledge reports within 5 business days and provide status updates during triage and remediation.
